About Me

Hello, I’m Karolina - UI/UX Designer between NY / NJ

From a young age, I’ve always had a passion for style and color. Moreover, as an obsessive organizer, I’ve always been compelled by simplicity, cleanliness and fine details. Before going to college, I spent my entire high school career immersed in Graphic Design. However, I decided to focus more attention on my analytical abilities, in order to pursue a Bachelors Degree in Business & Economics. By spending the last 6 years working in the beauty industry, I was able to grow my interpersonal skills in order to be an effective teammate and manager in sales.

Nonetheless, design is always on my mind. I enjoy working alongside creatives and projects that make a difference. By incorporating UI/UX in my day-to-day responsibilities, I’m able to incorporate both analytical and creative skills, to build product that has a purpose and find solutions to new and existing problems.

In my free-time, I love to try local coffee shops and energize my soul by hiking, dancing, playing board games and planning my next travel destination. As an avid traveler and first- generation Polish-American, I have great empathy and appreciation for different backgrounds, cultures, and views. I love to meet new people and immerse myself in experiences unknown to me - as my personal mantra is to “discover the unknown” (odkryj nieznane).
